### Overview
This dataset contains historical records of supply chain shipments with detailed information on pricing, logistics, quantities, vendors, and delivery timelines. Each record represents a single shipment, making the dataset suitable for exploratory data analysis (EDA) to understand shipment cost drivers, transportation patterns, and delivery performance.

The dataset reflects real-world supply chain operations and is commonly used for logistics, procurement, and cost optimization analysis.

---

### Dataset Size
- **Number of Rows:** 10,324  
- **Number of Columns:** 33  

Each row corresponds to an individual shipment, while each column represents a specific shipment-related attribute.

---

### Types of Data Included
The dataset consists of the following major categories of information:

#### 1. Shipment & Logistics Details
- Shipment mode (e.g., air, sea, road)
- Vendor or supplier information
- Destination country or region

#### 2. Pricing & Cost Information
- Unit price or shipment cost
- Quantity shipped
- Weight of shipments
- Cost-related fields that allow derivation of total shipment cost

#### 3. Time & Delivery Information
- Shipment dates
- Delivery dates
- Delivery duration (can be derived)

---

### Data Characteristics
- Structured tabular dataset
- Mix of categorical and numerical variables
- Contains real-world data inconsistencies such as missing values and varying formats, making it suitable for data cleaning and preprocessing exercises

---

### Use Case for EDA
This dataset is used to:
- Analyze shipment cost distribution
- Compare costs across shipment modes, countries, and vendors
- Identify trends in shipment volume and pricing over time
- Evaluate delivery performance and logistics efficiency

The dataset serves as the foundation for performing exploratory data analysis using Excel and Power BI in this project.
